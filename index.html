Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Through a nationwide digital ecosystem, Bangladesh 2.0 connects citizens to essential services and enhances digital literacy, ensuring equitable access to technological advancements.

Sustainability

With investments in solar and wind energy, Bangladesh 2.0 is driving the transition to eco-friendly power sources, reducing the nation’s carbon footprint while promoting sustainable growth.

Infrastructure

By modernizing transport, urban planning, and utilities, Bangladesh 2.0 aims to strengthen its global competitiveness, spur economic growth, and attract long-term investment.

Empowerment

By fostering economic opportunities and promoting inclusive development, Bangladesh 2.0 ensures that all citizens have the tools and resources to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.
